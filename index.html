The problem

Pet care today feels like guesswork.

Egyptian pet owners love their animals fiercely, but the system around them is opaque, scattered, and stressful.

01

You call a vet. They won't quote a price.

So you wait, and hope nothing gets worse.

02

Your dog's booster was… last March? Or was it April?

That crumpled paper booklet isn't helping.

03

Your neighbour recommended a clinic.

But you don't know their prices, their reviews, or if they even see rabbits.

The solution

PetBooked fixes all of that.

One calm, transparent app, so you can rest easy and your pet gets the care they deserve.

01

Prices before you book

See real costs across clinics before committing to a single visit.

02

Booking in 3 taps

Pick the vet, pick the time, done. No calls, no waiting on hold.

03

Smart health reminders

Vaccines, dewormers, annual checkups, all scheduled automatically per pet.

04

One profile per pet

Every visit, weight, vaccine, and note in one permanent, shareable record.
